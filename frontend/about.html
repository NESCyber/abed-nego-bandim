Biography

A Servant Leader of Bunkpurugu

Hon. Dr. Abed-Nego Lamangin Bandim is a distinguished scholar, administrator, and dedicated public servant representing the Bunkpurugu Constituency in the Parliament of Ghana. Center to the National Democratic Congress (NDC), Dr. Bandim is deeply committed to leveraging technology, improved infrastructure, and strong advocacy to drive socio-economic development across Bunkpurugu and the broader North East Region.

With a professional background spanning telecommunications regulation, academic research, and leadership in higher education, Dr. Bandim brings a wealth of policy and managerial expertise to public governance. As the Chairperson of the Information and Communications Committee in Parliament, he actively guides key legislative issues shaping the future of Ghana's digital economy.
